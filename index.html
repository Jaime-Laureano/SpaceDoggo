SPACE DOGGO

While out for a run one day, this poor lil' doggo ran straight into a mysterious portal.... sending him straight into outer space! Can you help this pup navigate back to planet Earth?

Move the mouse to control the spaceship. You'll need to collect 10 space friends in order to get back to Earth. Watch out for asteroids though!

YOU LOSE!!!!

Oh no, you've crash landed on an alien planet and are now doomed to live a life in the "Cone Of Shame".

YOU WIN!!!!

Congratulations on helping you furry friend guide his way safely back home.
